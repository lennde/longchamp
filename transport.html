Transport

Metro
Boissière: a 4 minute walk will get you to line 6.
Iéna: a 4 minute walk will get you to line 9.
Trocadero: a 4 minute walk will get you to both line 6 and 9.
Victor Hugo: a 10 minute walk will get you to line 2.

Parking
There is an underground Indigo parking facility at Avenue Kleber that you can reach in a 3 minute walk.
The location of the garage is 65 Av. Kléber, 75116 Paris.

Before parking the car you are able to drive up to the apartment building to unload your luggage.
