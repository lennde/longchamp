Heating
The heater is located in the room with the toilet:


The heater has two modes, one for hot water from the fossets and showers (mode 1), and another for heating the rooms through the radiators (mode 2).

To activate one or both modes, click on the 'Mode' button till the fosset icon appears (for mode 1) and/or the radiator icon (mode 2).
In the picture below you see that both the hot water from the fossets/shower (fosset icon) and the heating in the rooms (radiator icon) are activated.


To regulate the temperature you can use the respective knobs where the icon next to the knob indicates the radiator and the fosset icon.
In order to set the temperature for the room heating, you use the upper knob next to the radiator icon.
